Arts & Crafts

Context

The origins of Arts and Crafts go as far back as the 1860s in the United Kingdom. This was known as the arts and crafts movement, this movement involved decorative and fine arts and would eventually spread through the British Empire. There were three significant characteristics in the Arts & Crafts movement which includes natural qualities, designs, motifs inspired by nature, and “Vernacular designs” that focus on traditional techniques.

Existing Innovations

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe photoshop was first released on February 19th, 1990 and is a software program that is primarily used for creating images, paintings, graphic designs, and digital art.

Affinity Photo

A design software used for raw editing, digital art, and retouching

Autodesk Maya

Professional 3D animation software used for modeling, simulation, visual effects, computer animation, and 3D rendering for films.

iPad Pro

A tablet created by Apple, that runs on a M2 chip and comes with the latest updates. The iPad Pro can run multiple drawing softwares such as Procreate, Adobe Photoshop, Markup and more.

Crafts

Craft is a skill that involves making things by hand, many materials used include paper, textile, metal, wood, and washi. Some crafts include pottery, wood carving, and metalwork.
